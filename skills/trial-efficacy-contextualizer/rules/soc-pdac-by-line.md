# Standard of Care: Metastatic PDAC by Line

PDAC has limited SoC options and short median OS at every line. KRAS G12D is dominant (~40%), G12V (~30%), G12R (~15%), G12C (~1-2%).

## 1L mPDAC

- **FOLFIRINOX (mFOLFIRINOX)** — ORR 32%, mPFS 6.4 mo, mOS 11.1 mo (PRODIGE 4); for ECOG 0-1 fit patients
- **Gemcitabine + nab-paclitaxel (AG)** — ORR 23%, mPFS 5.5 mo, mOS 8.5 mo (MPACT); for less fit patients
- **NALIRIFOX** — NAPOLI-3 trial, mOS 11.1 mo vs AG 9.2 mo; emerging 1L option

## 2L mPDAC

### Post-FOLFIRINOX
- **AG (gemcitabine + nab-paclitaxel)** — switch backbone
- **Liposomal irinotecan (Onivyde) + 5-FU/LV** (NAPOLI-1) — ORR 16%, mPFS 3.1 mo, mOS 6.1 mo

### Post-AG
- **FOLFIRINOX or FOLFOX** — switch backbone
- **Liposomal irinotecan + 5-FU/LV**

### KRAS G12D mPDAC, 2L (emerging — Phase 1/2 only as of 2026)

- **MRTX1133** (Mirati): early Phase 1 ORR ~30-40%
- **GFH375 / fortemastinib** (innotech): Phase 1 ORR ~40%, mPFS ~5.5 mo (ESMO 2025 readout, n=66)
- **RMC-9805** (Revolution Medicines): Phase 1 multi-tumor, data immature

### KRAS G12C mPDAC (rare ~1-2%), 2L
- **Sotorasib** (CodeBreaK 100): PDAC cohort ORR ~21%, mPFS 4.0 mo
- **Adagrasib**: similar magnitude

### BRCA1/2-mutant or HRD mPDAC, 2L+
- **Olaparib maintenance** (POLO trial)

## 3L mPDAC

Limited options; consider clinical trials, best supportive care discussion. Median OS at 3L typically 3-4 months.

- Clinical trial enrollment is the preferred path when KPS allows
- Single-agent capecitabine, gemcitabine retreatment if not progressed during prior gem-based therapy
- Best supportive care + palliative care consultation

## Don't conflate

- KRAS G12D vs KRAS G12C drugs are NOT cross-active (covalent vs non-covalent vs ON-state, all mutation-selective)
- "RAS mutation (any)" trial enrollment doesn't mean drug is active in all RAS variants
- PRMT5 / MTAP-loss synthetic lethality is emerging in PDAC — separate from KRAS direct inhibition
