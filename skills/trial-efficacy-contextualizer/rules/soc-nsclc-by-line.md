# Standard of Care: Metastatic NSCLC by Line

Strategy depends on driver mutations (EGFR, ALK, ROS1, BRAF V600E, MET ex14, RET, NTRK, KRAS G12C, HER2 ex20), PD-L1 expression, and histology.

## EGFR-mutant NSCLC

### 1L (EGFR-mut, common L858R / Ex19del)

- **Osimertinib** ± chemo (FLAURA-2 added platinum/pemetrexed) — mPFS 19-25 mo, mOS 38 mo
- **Amivantamab + lazertinib** (MARIPOSA) — mPFS 23.7 mo (vs osimertinib 16.6 mo)

### 2L (post-osimertinib progression, no actionable resistance)

- **Platinum + pemetrexed** ± immunotherapy (limited PD-L1 benefit due to EGFR mutation)
- **Datopotamab deruxtecan (Dato-DXd)** — TROPION-Lung01 secondary endpoint
- **Patritumab deruxtecan (HER3-DXd)** — HERTHENA-Lung01 ORR ~30%, mPFS 5.5 mo
- **Amivantamab + chemo** (MARIPOSA-2)

### 2L (post-osimertinib, with C797S/MET amp resistance)

- C797S → 4th-gen EGFR-TKI trials (BLU-945, BLU-525)
- MET amp → EGFR-TKI + savolitinib/capmatinib

## ALK-positive NSCLC

### 1L
- **Alectinib** or **Lorlatinib** (CROWN trial: lorlatinib mPFS not reached at 5 yr)

### 2L
- Sequential next-gen ALK-TKI

## KRAS G12C NSCLC

### 1L
- Pembrolizumab ± chemo (PD-L1-driven)

### 2L (post-chemo+IO)
- **Sotorasib** (CodeBreaK 100/200): ORR 28-37%, mPFS 5.6 mo
- **Adagrasib** (KRYSTAL-1): ORR 43%, mPFS 6.5 mo
- **Divarasib** ORR 56%, mPFS 13.7 mo (Phase 1/2)

### 3L+
- Docetaxel ± ramucirumab
- Datopotamab deruxtecan
- Re-challenge with KRAS G12C inhibitor + chemo

## No actionable driver (KRAS WT, EGFR WT, ALK WT)

### 1L PD-L1 ≥50%
- Pembrolizumab monotherapy (KEYNOTE-024)
- Cemiplimab monotherapy

### 1L PD-L1 1-49%
- Pembrolizumab + chemo (KEYNOTE-189 non-squam, KEYNOTE-407 squam)

### 2L
- Docetaxel ± ramucirumab
- Pemetrexed (non-squam)

## Don't conflate

- Squamous vs non-squamous histology — pemetrexed contraindicated in squamous
- EGFR ex20 insertion: amivantamab, mobocertinib, sunvozertinib — distinct from common EGFR-mut paradigm
- MET ex14 vs MET amp — different drugs (capmatinib for ex14)
